Why We Need You

The aim to inspire through science and technology is a large one. Unfortunately, it can become quite expensive. The TechHOUNDS have to pay thousands of dollars to cover competition entry fees, travel dues, material cost, printing costs, etc. In addition, we could always use expertise in public relations, machining, building, project management, and other important skills. Through sponsorship and mentorship, you can watch as our team of students work to complete deadlines to not only acheive a goal in six weeks, but also a level of excellence and experience that will help us succeed past high school and in the workforce.
Benefits for Your Company

Not only will you have the satisfaction of donating your time, efforts, and monetary contributions to an organization that is inspiring a new generation, but your company will also receive recognition through various media. Depending on your level of sponsorship, your company name and/or logo (and description in the case of website) could be proudly displayed on our t-shirts, polos, website, newsletter, robot, and pit display. Through these means, your company would be seen by thousands of students and adults in different locations. Your company will also receive periodic updates during our build and competiton seasons, thus building a true partnership.
